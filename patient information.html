Patient Information

Registration Process: All new patients must register at the front desk or via our appointment form. Bring valid ID and previous medical records.
Visiting Hours: Daily from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Limited to two visitors per patient at a time.
Billing & Insurance: We accept all major insurance providers. Bills can be paid at the billing counter or online via our portal.
Medical Records: To request a copy of your records, visit the Records Office or email us at records@citycare.come.
Patient Rights: All patients have the right to respectful, confidential, and timely care, and to understand their treatment options.
Emergency Services: Available 24/7. In case of emergency, call +91 9911233611 or come directly to the Emergency Department.
